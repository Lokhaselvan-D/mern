Yara

Description

Written and directed by Yilmaz Arslan, the 1998 film tells the story of Hülya (Yelda Reynaud), the daughter of a working-class family who immigrated from Turkey to Germany and whose psychological problems made her send her to Turkey, and her efforts to heal and gain freedom.
